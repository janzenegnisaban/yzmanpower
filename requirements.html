What to prepare

Advance pooling with YZ Consultancy Services Inc. and YZ Manpower Services. Gather these before you apply or email your résumé.

Résumé & credentials

  • Updated résumé or biodata
  • TOR, diploma, and certificates where applicable
  • Proof of eligibility for the role (licenses, NC, etc.)

Civil & clearance documents

  • PSA birth certificate (and dependents’, if applicable)
  • Police and/or NBI clearance (as required)

Government & benefits

  • SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and TIN (or application status)
  • Two valid government-issued IDs

Health & readiness

  • Medical result, if already available

How to submit

Complete the questionnaire on Job openings and follow the email steps, or send your documents directly to yz.manpowerservices@gmail.com. For hiring inquiries, use Contact.
