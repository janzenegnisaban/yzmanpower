About Us

Built on Trust,
Driven by Service

YZ Manpower Services is a licensed manpower agency based in the Central Business District of the Subic Bay Freeport Zone. We specialize in providing skilled, semi-skilled, and professional workforce solutions to businesses within and beyond the freeport zone.

Our commitment is simple: Service First, Customer First. We take time to understand your business needs and deliver tailored manpower solutions that help you grow and operate efficiently.
